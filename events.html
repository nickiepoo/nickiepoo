Stand-up For Beginners

A four week introduction to Stand-up Comedy practise for absolute beginners

Tues 16th Sept 2025
Tues 23rd Sept 2025
Tues 30th Sept 2025
Tues 7th Sept 2025

Do you think you’re funny? Looking for a new challenge? Maybe you just love to laugh! If you’ve ever wanted to have a go at stand-up comedy in a friendly and supportive environment, this is the course for you. In these four weeks we’ll prepare you for your first gig, exploring your identity, stage presence, set structure, joke writing and improvisation. Stand-up for Funny People

An eight week stand-up comedy course that will let you get your teeth into who you are as a stand-up comedy performer.

Tues 21st Oct 2025
Tues 28th Oct 2025
Tues 4th Nov 2025
Tues 11th Nov 2025
Tues 18th Nov 2025
Tues 25th Nov 2025
Tues 2nd Dec 2025
Tues 9th Dec 2025

Strictly limited to ten places to allow for everyone to get plenty of mic time and culminating in a LIVE SHOW for friends and family, this course is an intensive exploration of stand-up comedy practice. It is suitable for beginners who are willing to jump in head first but will particularly benefit those with a little stage time under their belt. Seasoned professionals will be encourage to move out of their comfort zones and challenge themselves in new ways, pushing everyone to explore the boundaries of stand-up comedy.
